Ople is for you.

Ople is a new app where pk-12 educators are the experts — sharing best practices, insights, strategies, and ideas through short-form video.

Ople places the learning teachers want at their fingertips, in 1-5 minute videos made by teachers for teachers down the hall, across the country, and beyond.

Key App Features

  • Search for and discover new ideas
  • Share, save, and organize unlimited videos
  • Engage and collaborate with peers anywhere

Ople is for any pk-12 educator, school, or district — for ongoing, active, bite-sized professional learning, spotlighting excellence or calibrating curriculum, or the dissemination of new and novel ideas.

Ople's mobile platform, verified educator community, and library of teacher-produced and vetted videos will be available for individual and school-based adoption in the 2024–2025 school year.
